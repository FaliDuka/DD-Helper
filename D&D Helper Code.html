Welcome to Jimbo's Market!

This is an example website for an on-line store that sells an electric mix of products. The site includes a catalog of products and a facility for creating and maintaining customer accounts, complete with a shipping cart for "ordering" items.

Please note that this is not an actual on-line store! It is just an example - designed for a web programming course.

The navigation bar at the top of the page allows a visitor to browse items in the store by category, or to search for a product by name. In order to " place an order", however, a customer account is required.

Enjoy your "shopping" experience!
